SDA => Digital Pin 10
SCK => Digital Pin 13
MOSI => Digital Pin 11
MISO => Digital Pin 12
GND => GND (you can connect this either directly to a GND Pin or to a negative point on the power rail)
IRQ => ***NOT USED***
RST => Digital Pin 9
3.3V => 3.3V Pin (DO NOT CONNECT IT TO 5V AS THIS WILL DAMAGE THE READER)
